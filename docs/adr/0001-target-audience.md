# Target audience: platform customers and Perforce field teams

This repo's skills and commands are designed for two audiences: (1) **platform customers** — QA
and performance engineers who already have BlazeMeter/Perfecto accounts and want to extract more
value, faster — and (2) **Perforce field teams** (sales engineering, support, customer success)
who use them on customers' behalf and will be the earliest adopters.

We explicitly do **not** target the broad public / newcomers with no platform access as a v1
audience. We therefore assume the reader has working accounts and baseline domain fluency, and we
do not pay the cost of explaining the platforms from zero. The repo may be public, but
onboarding-someone-from-nothing is a non-goal for now.
